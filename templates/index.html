Find Likely Patient Zeros

Our algorithm uses local node probability weighting and levels of back-tracing to nominate the top nodes with the highest probability of being patient zero. 


The weights and biases were determined using a "NEAT" algorithm, which uses natural selection. See more about NeuroEvolution of Augmenting Topologies (NEAT) here .
